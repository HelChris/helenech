Welcome, I'm Helene,

I am currently pursuing a degree in Front-End Development at Noroff Vocational School, while working full-time as a Kindergarten teacher. My journey into web development began with a high school project where I created a "newspaper" webpage. Although it was a short-lived project that only existed locally, it sparked my interest in web development.

After graduating from high school with a focus on Media and Communication, I initially aimed to become something like a journalist or photographer and display my work online. However, I found myself in a "temporary" job at a kindergarten, which led to a bachelor's degree and eventually a decade-long career in early childhood education. During this time, I returned to university to further my studies, focusing on training future kindergarten teachers.

Through these experiences, I discovered a passion for continuous learning and decided to return to my interest in programming and web development. Today, I am dedicated to building innovative and responsive web applications. I take pride in writing semantic HTML and am committed to mastering CSS, despite our current love/hate relationship.

I am excited about the future and look forward to creating impactful and user-friendly web solutions.
